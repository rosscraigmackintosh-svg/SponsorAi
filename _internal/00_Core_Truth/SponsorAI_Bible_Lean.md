## 1. SponsorAI Bible (Lean)

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Authority: Strategic

---

1. Product Definition

SponsorAI is a sponsorship intelligence platform that provides structured visibility into the sports sponsorship landscape.

It helps:

- brands explore where attention exists 

- properties understand their market position 

- both sides navigate opportunities with clarity 

---

2. Category Positioning

SponsorAI is best understood as:

&gt; a market visibility and decision-support layer for sponsorship.

It sits:

- above raw social data 

- below commercial execution 

- beside CRM and activation tools 

It is not a deal platform.

---

3. Core User Surfaces

Primary experience areas:

- Explore (curated discovery) 

- Browse All (structured grid) 

- Property Pages 

- Watchlist 

- Compare 

- Portfolio 

- Opportunities 

Experience philosophy:

- exploration over prescription 

- signals over scores 

- context over conclusions 

---

4. Experience Tone

The product should feel:

- calm 

- modern 

- confident 

- slightly editorial 

- intelligence-led 

Avoid:

- sports betting energy 

- fantasy league aesthetics 

- gamification mechanics 

- aggressive growth language 

---

5. Property in Focus Philosophy

The hero surface is:

- data-triggered 

- deterministic 

- not editorial promotion 

- not paid placement 

Purpose:

&gt; surface notable movement in the landscape.

---

6. Opportunities Positioning

Opportunities provide structured visibility into active sponsorship openings.

They must:

- remain secondary to FanScore 

- remain neutral in tone 

- avoid recommendation framing 

- include minimum investment clarity 

They must not behave like:

- deal marketplaces 

- lead-gen funnels 

- performance predictions 

---

7. Initial Market Focus

Current wedge:

- Motorsport 

- Mid-tier density 

- Under-served sponsorship layers 

Rationale:

- high fragmentation 

- strong sponsor demand 

- limited existing intelligence tools 

Expansion is possible but not assumed.

---

8. Data Reality

Current strength:

- social media attention signals 

Known gaps:

- broadcast exposure 

- full commercial valuation 

- offline activation impact 

The product must remain honest about these limits.

---

9. Long-Term Direction

SponsorAI aims to become:

- the trusted intelligence layer for sponsorship 

- the neutral visibility standard 

- the calm thinking surface for brands and properties 

It does **not** aim to become:

- a trading platform 

- a deal marketplace 

- an ROI prediction engine 

---

End of Bible
