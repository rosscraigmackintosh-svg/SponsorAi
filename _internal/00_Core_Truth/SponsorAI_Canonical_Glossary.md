## 1. SponsorAI — Canonical Glossary

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Authority: High (language consistency)

---

1. Purpose

Defines the canonical terminology used across SponsorAI to ensure:

- consistent language 

- clear cross-team communication 

- stable AI interpretation 

- reduced semantic drift 

Primary goal:

&gt; One concept = one preferred term.

Where conflicts arise, this glossary provides the preferred wording.

---

2. Core Product Terms

SponsorAI

The intelligence platform providing structured visibility into the sponsorship landscape.

Preferred framing:

&gt; sponsorship intelligence platform 

&gt; market visibility layer 

&gt; decision-support surface 

Avoid:

- marketplace 

- broker 

- deal engine 

---

Property

A canonical entity within SponsorAI.

Supported types:

- Athlete / Driver 

- Team 

- Event 

- Series / Championship 

A Property is the primary unit of analysis.

---

Brand

A commercial entity exploring sponsorship opportunities.

Brands interact primarily through:

- Explore 

- FitScore 

- Portfolio 

- Opportunities 

---

3. Signal Definitions

FanScore

**Definition**

Measures digital attention gravity around a property.

**Represents**

- audience scale 

- engagement quality 

- growth momentum 

**Does NOT represent**

- value 

- ROI 

- quality 

- sponsorship success 

Canonical short phrase:

&gt; “FanScore reflects attention, not value.”

---

FitScore

**Definition**

Measures contextual alignment between a brand and a property.

**Represents**

- geographic alignment 

- category compatibility 

- budget compatibility 

**Does NOT represent**

- popularity 

- performance 

- expected return 

Canonical short phrase:

&gt; “FitScore reflects alignment, not outcome.”

---

Budget / Minimum Entry

**Definition**

Represents financial accessibility to a sponsorship opportunity.

**Role**

- gating filter 

- compatibility signal 

**Not used for**

- ranking 

- scoring 

- optimisation 

---

4. Experience Surfaces

Explore

The primary discovery surface.

Characteristics:

- row-based 

- deterministic 

- market-led 

- non-personalised at core 

Purpose:

&gt; Enable structured discovery across the landscape.

---

Browse All

Structured grid view of the full property universe within filters.

Purpose:

&gt; Provide complete visibility when needed.

---

Property Page

The canonical detail surface for a property.

Contains:

- FanScore 

- contextual signals 

- opportunities 

- descriptive overview 

---

Watchlist

User-curated collection of properties for lightweight tracking.

Nature:

- explicit 

- user-controlled 

- high-trust signal 

---

Compare

Structured side-by-side evaluation surface.

Purpose:

&gt; Support analytical evaluation without declaring winners.

---

Portfolio

User’s active sponsorship set or serious consideration set.

Represents higher intent than Watchlist.

---

Opportunities

Structured sponsorship openings declared by properties.

Key rule:

&gt; Opportunities expose availability — they do not recommend action.

---

5. Confidence Language

High Confidence

Strong data coverage and freshness.

Preferred phrasing:

- “based on strong data coverage”

---

Moderate Confidence

Reasonable but incomplete coverage.

Preferred phrasing:

- “based on available data”

---

Limited Confidence

Coverage gaps present.

Preferred phrasing:

- “limited data coverage”

---

6. Personalisation Terms

Explicit Signals

User-controlled actions such as:

- Watchlist additions 

- Compare selections 

- Portfolio entries 

Highest-trust personal inputs.

---

Session Context

Temporary behavioural context within a single session.

Characteristics:

- short-lived 

- reversible 

- non-authoritative 

---

Related to Your Watchlist

Permitted personalisation surface derived from explicit signals.

Must remain:

- explainable 

- bounded 

- non-dominant 

---

7. Governance Terms

Constitution

The highest authority document defining non-negotiable principles.

Overrides all other product docs.

---

Working Memory

Living snapshot of current product reality and recent decisions.

High update frequency.

---

Core Truth

Immutable foundational doctrine.

Rarely changed.

---

Control Room

Internal operational surface for monitoring:

- model health 

- coverage 

- pipeline status 

- confidence distribution 

---

8. Language Guardrails

Preferred Tone

- calm 

- analytical 

- grounded 

- neutral 

- intelligence-first 

---

Avoided Language

Do not use:

- “best sponsorship” 

- “guaranteed ROI” 

- “top performing” 

- “winning property” 

- hype or betting metaphors
