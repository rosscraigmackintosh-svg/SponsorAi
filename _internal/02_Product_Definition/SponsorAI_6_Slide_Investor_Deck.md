## 1. SponsorAI — 6-Slide Investor Deck

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Audience: Investor 

Authority: High (fundraising narrative)

---

Slide 1 — The Problem

Headline

The sponsorship market is large but structurally opaque — especially below the elite tier.

Key Points

- Discovery is fragmented 

- Mid-tier properties are hard to evaluate 

- Brands rely on manual research and relationships 

- Existing tools focus on media value, not landscape clarity 

- The long tail of sponsorship remains inefficient 

Speaker Note

Anchor the room in inefficiency, not lack of data.

---

Slide 2 — The Insight

Headline

The market doesn’t need more optimisation. It needs visibility.

Core Narrative

Most platforms try to:

- predict ROI 

- rank winners 

- broker deals 

SponsorAI takes a different stance:

&gt; Better visibility → better human decisions.

Speaker Note

This is the philosophical wedge. Keep it calm and confident.

---

Slide 3 — The Solution

Headline

SponsorAI is the visibility layer for the sponsorship market.

Visual (recommended)

Show system diagram or product hero.

Core Model

Three independent signals:

- **FanScore — Attention** 

- **FitScore — Alignment** 

- **Budget — Access**

Hard Principle

These signals are deliberately never collapsed into a single score.

Speaker Note

Investors should feel the discipline here.

---

Slide 4 — Product Experience

Headline

Structured discovery replaces fragmented research.

Show

- Explore screen 

- Property page 

- Compare flow 

- Opportunities surface 

Key Message

SponsorAI helps users answer:

&gt; “Is this property worth exploring?”

—not—

&gt; “What deal should I do?”

Speaker Note

This slide should feel very product-led.

---

Slide 5 — Why Now

Headline

The infrastructure for attention exists. The interpretation layer does not.

Supporting Points

- Explosion of mid-tier sports properties 

- Social signal richness has matured 

- Brands demand more measurable sponsorship 

- Market below elite tier remains under-served 

- Existing tools are misaligned with discovery needs 

Speaker Note

This is your timing argument.

---

Slide 6 — Business Model &amp; Vision

Headline

Customers pay for clarity — not influence.

Revenue Streams

- subscription access 

- advanced intelligence features 

- property premium profiles 

- data / API access (future)

Long-Term Vision

&gt; Become the trusted visibility layer for the global sponsorship market.

Closing Tone

Calm. Inevitable. Not hypey.

---

End of Document
