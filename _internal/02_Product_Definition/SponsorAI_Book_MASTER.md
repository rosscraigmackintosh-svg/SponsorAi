Part I — Why This Exists

1. Market Reality

SponsorAI exists because sponsorship decisions increasingly require institutional defensibility.

Primary Economic Buyer:

- Global CMOs

- Heads of Sponsorship

- Procurement Directors

- CFOs requiring audit proof

Not built for:

- Agencies optimising for speed

- Small brands without governance pressure

- Properties seeking leverage

Core trigger:

"Show me how this was selected."

SponsorAI becomes essential when sponsorship selection must withstand board-level scrutiny.

---

2. The Structural Problem

Sponsorship decisions combine two fundamentally different questions:

1. Do fans care?

2. Does this fit my scenario?

Most tools collapse these into one score. SponsorAI separates them.

---

3. SponsorAI Definition

SponsorAI is a neutral decision-support system structured around:

- FanScore (engagement health, scenario-agnostic)

- FitScore (future phase, scenario-specific and private)

SponsorAI is:

- Neutral

- Explainable

- Versioned

- Governance-first

SponsorAI is not:

- A marketplace

- A deal broker

- A leaderboard

- A pay-to-play platform

- An ROI guarantee engine

Part III — FanScore Model

Scope Boundary

FanScore v1 = Digital-only signals.

Not included:

- TV reach

- Offline attendance

- Revenue

- Sponsorship pricing

Any expansion requires version change.

Purpose

FanScore measures fan engagement health.

It does not represent value, fit, or ROI.

Signal Groups

- Audience &amp; Reach

- Engagement Quality

- Loyalty &amp; Stability

- Cultural Resonance

- Integrity Checks

Design Rules

- Engagement &gt; raw reach

- Smoothing prevents viral distortion

- Confidence always displayed

- Anti-abuse detection active

- No commercial influence

FanScore cannot be exported as:

- “Best property”

- “Top opportunity”

- ROI proxy

---

Part IV — FitScore (Future Phase)

Status: Planned capability (not in MVP)

Purpose

FitScore measures alignment between a property and a defined user scenario.

Scenario inputs may include:

- Objectives

- Geography

- Timing

- Category

- Activation capability

- Risk appetite

- Optional budget

FitScore rules:

- Private only

- Scenario-bound

- Not exportable as ranking

- Cannot generate public leaderboards

- Confidence always shown

Uncertainty Flag:

FitScore weighting finalisation requires future governance review.

---

Part V — Dual Metric Governance

- No total combined score.

- No default ranking.

- FitScore never global.

- Sorting must be explicit and labelled.

- Screenshot test applied to all UI.
