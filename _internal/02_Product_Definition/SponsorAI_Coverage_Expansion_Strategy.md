## 1. SponsorAI — Coverage Expansion Strategy

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Authority: Strategic

---

1. Purpose

Defines how SponsorAI expands coverage without diluting trust or data quality.

Primary goal:

&gt; Depth before breadth.

---

2. Phase Strategy

Phase 1 — Motorsport (Current Wedge)

Focus:

- mid-tier density 

- high sponsorship activity 

- strong social presence 

Success criteria:

- stable FanScore distribution 

- strong property coverage 

- reliable ingestion pipeline 

---

Phase 2 — Adjacent High-Sponsorship Sports

Candidates:

- Rugby 

- Cycling 

- Combat sports 

- Esports 

Entry requirements:

- sufficient social signal 

- fragmented sponsorship landscape 

- clear sponsor demand 

---

Phase 3 — Broad Multi-Sport Coverage

Only after:

- ingestion proven at scale 

- confidence framework stable 

- drift monitoring mature 

---

3. Sport Readiness Checklist

Before entering a new sport:

Must evaluate:

- social platform density 

- handle discoverability 

- sponsorship activity level 

- tier structure clarity 

- data volatility risk 

If weak, delay entry.

---

4. Property Density Thresholds

SponsorAI should avoid thin markets.

Heuristic:

&gt; Avoid launching a sport with insufficient mid-tier depth.

Purpose:

- preserve Explore richness 

- maintain discovery value 

- avoid empty shelves 

---

5. Quality Gates

Expansion must not degrade:

- FanScore stability 

- confidence distribution 

- ingestion reliability 

- identity resolution accuracy 

If any metric regresses → pause expansion.

---

6. Geographic Expansion

Initial bias:

- UK / Europe motorsport ecosystem 

Future expansion may include:

- North America 

- APAC 

- emerging motorsport markets 

But must remain:

- data-led 

- demand-led 

- trust-first 

---

7. Commercial Alignment

Coverage expansion should favour markets with:

- active sponsorship spend 

- mid-market brand participation 

- fragmented discovery 

Avoid markets that are:

- fully broker-dominated 

- extremely opaque 

- data-poor 

---

8. Guardrails

SponsorAI must not:

- expand purely for vanity scale 

- sacrifice data quality for breadth 

- imply full market coverage prematurely 

---

End of Document
