## 1. SponsorAI — Investor One-Pager

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Audience: Investor / Strategic 

Authority: High (external narrative)

---

The Problem

The global sponsorship market is large but structurally opaque — especially below the elite tier.

Today:

- discovery is fragmented 

- mid-tier properties are hard to evaluate 

- brands rely on manual research and relationships 

- most tools focus on media valuation, not landscape clarity 

As a result, the mid-market remains inefficient and under-served.

---

The Insight

The market does not primarily need another marketplace or optimisation engine.

It needs visibility.

&gt; Better visibility → better human decisions.

SponsorAI is built on the belief that the missing layer in sponsorship is structured, neutral intelligence that makes the landscape legible.

---

The Solution

SponsorAI is a sponsorship intelligence platform that provides a clear, structured view of the sports sponsorship ecosystem.

The platform is built on three independent signals:

- **FanScore (Attention)** — measures digital audience gravity 

- **FitScore (Alignment)** — measures contextual compatibility 

- **Budget / Minimum (Access)** — represents financial feasibility 

These signals remain deliberately separate.

---

Why This Matters

Most existing tools:

- optimise 

- rank 

- predict ROI 

- or broker deals 

SponsorAI takes a different position.

&gt; It clarifies the market rather than attempting to decide the outcome.

This creates a more trustworthy foundation for long-term decision-making.

---

Initial Wedge

SponsorAI is initially focused on:

- motorsport 

- mid-tier density 

- social-first attention signals 

This segment is:

- commercially active 

- structurally fragmented 

- and poorly served by existing tools 

---

Product Experience

Core surfaces include:

- Explore — structured market discovery 

- Property Pages — canonical entity view 

- Watchlist / Compare — evaluation workflow 

- Portfolio — active sponsorship set 

- Opportunities — declared sponsorship openings 

The experience is intentionally calm, analytical, and non-gamified.

---

Trust as a Product Feature

SponsorAI is deliberately designed to avoid:

- pay-to-rank mechanics 

- marketplace incentives 

- black-box recommendations 

- ROI promises 

- hype-driven dashboards 

The platform maintains:

- signal separation 

- deterministic discovery 

- confidence-aware analytics 

- commercial neutrality 

This restraint is a core strategic moat.

---

Business Model

SponsorAI monetises through:

- subscription access 

- advanced intelligence features 

- property-side premium profiles 

- data and API access (future)

Importantly:

&gt; Customers pay for clarity — not influence.

---

Long-Term Vision

SponsorAI aims to become:

&gt; the trusted visibility layer for the global sponsorship market.

The ambition is not to replace human judgement, but to make the landscape finally understandable at scale.
