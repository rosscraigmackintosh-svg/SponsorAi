## 1. SponsorAI — Methodology Overview

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Audience: External / Investor / Partner

---

What SponsorAI Does

SponsorAI provides a structured, neutral view of the sports sponsorship landscape.

It helps brands and properties understand:

- where audience attention exists 

- how properties are evolving 

- where contextual alignment may be present 

SponsorAI is a **decision-support layer**, not a deal platform.

---

The Three-Signal Model

SponsorAI is built on strict signal separation.

FanScore — Attention

Measures the scale and momentum of digital audience attention.

Based on:

- audience scale 

- engagement quality 

- growth momentum 

FanScore indicates visibility — **not value or ROI**.

---

FitScore — Alignment

Measures contextual compatibility between a brand and a property.

Based on:

- geographic overlap 

- category alignment 

- budget compatibility 

FitScore indicates relevance — **not performance or expected return**.

---

Budget / Minimum Entry — Access

Represents financial feasibility only.

Budget acts as:

- a gating filter 

- a compatibility indicator 

It is **not part of scoring**.

---

How SponsorAI Stays Neutral

SponsorAI is deliberately designed to avoid optimisation bias.

Key safeguards:

- no composite master score 

- no pay-to-rank dynamics 

- no ROI predictions 

- no “best sponsorship” rankings 

- no marketplace brokering 

The platform surfaces visibility — users retain judgement.

---

Data Foundations

SponsorAI currently focuses on:

- cross-platform social attention signals 

- engagement behaviour 

- growth dynamics 

The system is transparent about known gaps, including:

- broadcast exposure 

- full commercial terms 

- offline activation impact 

---

Confidence &amp; Transparency

SponsorAI expresses confidence based on:

- data coverage 

- signal freshness 

- stability of engagement 

Where data is limited, the platform softens language and avoids false precision.

---

Why This Matters

The sponsorship market remains fragmented and opaque.

SponsorAI’s goal is simple:

&gt; Provide a calm, structured view of the landscape so better human decisions can follow.

It does not attempt to replace judgement — only to support it.
