## 1. SponsorAI — Positioning Narrative

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Audience: Investor / Partner / Strategic

---

1. Category Definition

SponsorAI is a **sponsorship intelligence platform** that provides structured visibility into the sports sponsorship landscape.

It is best understood as:

&gt; the thinking layer between raw audience data and commercial sponsorship decisions.

---

2. The Problem

The sponsorship market is large but structurally opaque.

Today:

- discovery is fragmented 

- mid-tier properties are hard to evaluate 

- brands lack structured comparability 

- most decisions rely on relationships or manual research 

- existing tools focus on media valuation, not landscape clarity 

The result is inefficient matching and missed opportunities across the mid-market.

---

3. The Insight

Most platforms try to optimise decisions.

SponsorAI takes a different stance:

&gt; Better visibility leads to better human judgement.

Instead of predicting outcomes or ranking winners, SponsorAI focuses on making the landscape legible.

---

4. The Solution

SponsorAI provides a structured intelligence layer built on three independent signals:

- FanScore — attention 

- FitScore — alignment 

- Budget / Minimum — access 

Together, these allow users to explore the market with clarity while retaining decision control.

---

5. Why Now

Several shifts make this moment attractive:

- explosion of mid-tier sports properties 

- growing social signal richness 

- increasing sponsor demand for measurable visibility 

- fragmentation below the elite tier 

- rising comfort with intelligence-led decision support 

The infrastructure for attention measurement now exists — but the interpretation layer is missing.

---

6. Strategic Positioning

SponsorAI deliberately avoids becoming:

- a marketplace 

- a deal broker 

- an ROI prediction engine 

- a pay-to-rank platform 

- a hype analytics dashboard 

This restraint is intentional and trust-driven.

---

7. Initial Wedge

SponsorAI is initially focused on:

- motorsport 

- mid-tier density 

- under-served sponsorship layers 

This provides:

- strong signal availability 

- high commercial relevance 

- manageable ecosystem scope 

---

8. Long-Term Vision

SponsorAI aims to become:

&gt; the trusted visibility standard for the global sponsorship market.

The goal is not to replace human judgement, but to make the landscape finally understandable at scale.
