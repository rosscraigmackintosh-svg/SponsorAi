## Density Philosophy

SponsorAI uses two density modes:

### Compact (Default for App Surfaces)

Used for dashboards, tables, and analytical views.

- tighter vertical rhythm  
- information-dense but readable  
- controlled whitespace  
- data prioritised  

Compact must never feel cramped.

---

### Comfortable (Marketing, Empty States, Onboarding)

- more breathing room  
- softer vertical spacing  
- editorial rhythm  

Density modes must not mix within the same surface.
