# SponsorAI Visual Philosophy  
Version: v2.1  
Status: Active  

SponsorAI is a calm, analytical B2B SaaS platform.

It is not a marketing site.
It is not a sports brand.
It is not a gamified dashboard.

It must feel:

- calm  
- structured  
- deliberate  
- quietly premium  
- trust-first  
- evidence-led  

Clarity is the product.

---

## Core Rule

Accent is punctuation, not paint.

Accent highlights intent.
It does not decorate surfaces.

If a surface looks “designed”, reduce it.
If something draws attention without meaning, remove it.

---

## Visual Guardrails

- Legibility first  
- Structure over flourish  
- Borders before shadows  
- Neutral base, restrained warmth  
- One accent, used intentionally  
- Data before decoration  

Avoid:

- loud gradients  
- saturated fills  
- progress rings  
- gauge or speedometer visuals  
- rainbow chart palettes  
- decorative motion  

When uncertain, simplify.

---

## Behavioural Tone

SponsorAI should feel like:

A well-lit analytical workspace.
A calm research environment.
A structured decision tool.

It must not feel like:

A trading terminal.
A sports broadcast graphic.
A leaderboard.
A marketing funnel.

---

## Reduction Principle

When something feels off:

1. Reduce saturation  
2. Reduce motion  
3. Reduce visual weight  
4. Increase spacing  
5. Increase hierarchy clarity  

Restraint is a feature, not a limitation.
