# Tables and Comparison Density Contract

Tables and comparison views are core analytical surfaces in SponsorAI.

They must:

- support fast cross-property scanning  
- preserve analytical credibility  
- maintain calm visual tone  
- avoid visual clutter  

When in doubt, favour clarity over compression.

---

## Row Density

### Compact Mode (Default)

- row height: tight but breathable  
- vertical padding: space.2 to space.3  
- horizontal padding: space.3 to space.4  
- typography: text.sm  

### Comfortable Mode

- increase vertical padding one step  
- maintain consistent rhythm  

Do not mix density modes in the same table.

---

## Header Rules

Column headers must:

- use text.sm  
- weight 500 or 600  
- muted or primary text colour  
- no heavy fills  

Avoid strong header backgrounds.

---

## Row Styling

Body rows must:

- use neutral background  
- avoid strong zebra striping  
- use subtle hover shift only  

No glow effects.
No heavy highlight colours.

---

## Alignment Rules

- text left aligned  
- numbers right aligned  
- actions right aligned  

Avoid centre alignment for core data.

---

## Behaviour Guardrails

Tables must not:

- pulse on updates  
- reshuffle aggressively  
- animate dramatically  
- flash on refresh  

Subtle numeric transitions are acceptable.
