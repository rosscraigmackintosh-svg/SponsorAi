---

# Interactive State Rules

Interactive elements must feel calm, precise, and controlled.

---

## Primary Buttons

- use accent colour  
- text must meet WCAG AA  
- hover: subtle contrast increase  
- active: minor press effect  
- disabled: reduced contrast, no hover  

---

## Secondary Buttons

- neutral surface or outline  
- subtle border  
- primary text colour  

---

## Focus States

- visible focus ring required  
- use accent or strong border  
- never remove focus outline  

---

## Disabled Philosophy

Disabled elements must:

- reduce contrast  
- remove hover affordance  
- avoid saturated fills  
- clearly appear non-interactive  

---

## Motion Rules

Avoid:

- aggressive transitions  
- bouncing indicators  
- animated ranking effects  

Motion must be subtle and purposeful.
