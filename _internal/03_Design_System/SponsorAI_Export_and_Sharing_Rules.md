## 1. SponsorAI — Export & Sharing Rules

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Authority: High (presentation integrity)

---

1. Purpose

Defines how SponsorAI data and artefacts can be shared externally while preserving trust, neutrality, and methodological clarity.

---

2. Export Principles

All exports must be:

- neutral in tone 

- clearly attributed 

- snapshot-based 

- free from optimisation claims 

- free from ranking language 

Exports represent **structured visibility**, not recommendations.

---

3. Attribution Requirements

Where SponsorAI data appears externally, include:

&gt; “Measured by SponsorAI”

or approved equivalent.

Attribution must be:

- visible 

- understated 

- non-promotional 

---

4. Snapshot Behaviour

Shared views must be:

- frozen at time of export 

- version-stamped 

- dataset-bounded 

- reproducible 

Users must understand they are viewing a point-in-time snapshot.

---

5. Share Link Rules

Public share views must:

- be read-only 

- contain no personal user data 

- contain no private portfolio signals 

- preserve neutral framing 

- avoid recommendation language 

---

6. Co-Branding Rules

Co-branded exports are allowed with:

- property logo 

- brand logo 

- SponsorAI attribution 

But must never imply:

- endorsement 

- partnership brokerage 

- performance guarantee 

---

7. Prohibited Export Patterns

Exports must never include:

- “best sponsorship” language 

- ROI promises 

- optimisation claims 

- pay-to-rank indicators 

- hidden boosts 

---

8. Version &amp; Methodology Visibility

Where appropriate, exports should include:

- methodology reference 

- dataset coverage note 

- version stamp 

This reinforces analytical trust.

---

9. Future Safeguard

If new export formats are introduced, they must pass:

- neutrality check 

- trust check 

- signal separation check 

- marketplace boundary check 

---
