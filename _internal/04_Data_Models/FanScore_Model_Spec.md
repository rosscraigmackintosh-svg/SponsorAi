## 1. FanScore Model SpecificationVersion: v1 DigitalStatus: Active

---

1. Purpose

FanScore measures fan engagement health.

It does NOT measure:

- Sponsorship value

- Brand fit

- ROI

- Price

---

2. Scope Boundary

Digital-only inputs.

Excluded:

- TV ratings

- Offline attendance

- Revenue

- Sponsorship cost

Expansion requires version change.

---

3. Signal Groups

A. Audience &amp; Reach 

B. Engagement Quality 

C. Loyalty &amp; Stability 

D. Cultural Resonance 

E. Integrity Signals 

---

4. Weighting Logic

- Engagement quality &gt; raw reach

- Loyalty dampens volatility

- Reach normalized

- Bot/integrity penalties applied

Exact weighting coefficients: INTERNAL (uncertainty flagged for publication decision)

---

5. Smoothing Rules

- Time-window averaging

- Spike dampening

- Viral event moderation

---

6. Confidence Score

Confidence reflects:

- Data completeness

- Data freshness

- Stability

- Anomaly detection

Confidence is descriptive, not predictive.

---

7. Anti-Abuse Logic

Triggers:

- Engagement anomalies

- Suspicious growth

- Pattern inconsistencies

Penalties are visible and explained.

---

8. Forbidden Uses

FanScore cannot be used as:

- Universal ranking

- Best-in-class badge

- ROI proxy

- Sponsorship value estimate

---

9. Tie Logic

If FanScores close:

- Higher stability preferred

- Higher confidence preferred

- No commercial override
