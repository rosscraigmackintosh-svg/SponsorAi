## 1. FitScore Model SpecificationStatus: Future Phase (Conceptual Governance Model)Not active in MVP

---

1. Purpose

FitScore measures scenario-based alignment between:

- A property

- A defined user scenario

---

2. Scenario Inputs (Illustrative)

- Objective

- Geography

- Timing

- Target audience

- Category

- Activation capability

- Risk tolerance

- Optional budget

---

3. Minimum Required Inputs

Must include:

- Objective

- Geography

- Category

- Activation capability

Missing inputs lower confidence.

---

4. Dimensions

- Audience alignment

- Geographic match

- Category alignment

- Activation feasibility

- Timing relevance

- Risk compatibility

Dimension weighting follows scenario objectives.

---

5. Weighting Rules

- Scenario-driven

- Not user-adjustable in ways that enable leaderboard reconstruction

- No universal weighting set

Final calibration pending (uncertainty flagged).

---

6. Confidence

Displayed clearly.

Explains:

- Missing inputs

- Data limitations

- Ambiguity areas

---

7. Hard Rules

- Private only

- Scenario-bound

- Not exportable as ranking

- Cannot power global sorting

- Cannot create public comparison tables

---

8. Edge Cases

- Low-data properties

- Cross-market objectives

- Tradeoff surfacing mandatory

---

9. Forbidden Uses

Cannot be:

- Published

- Used by properties for pitching

- Used for cross-scenario ranking
