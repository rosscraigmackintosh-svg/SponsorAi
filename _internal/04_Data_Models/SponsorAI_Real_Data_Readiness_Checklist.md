## 1. SponsorAI — Real Data Readiness Checklist

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Authority: Operational

---

1. Purpose

Defines the minimum bar before SponsorAI data is considered production-ready.

Primary goal:

&gt; Prevent premature scale on weak data foundations.

---

2. Property Coverage Readiness

Minimum expectations:

- canonical IDs stable 

- major duplicates resolved 

- core motorsport entities covered 

- tier distribution reasonable 

- no major identity collisions 

---

3. Platform Coverage

Before broad release, confirm:

- Instagram coverage strong 

- X/Twitter coverage acceptable 

- YouTube where relevant 

- TikTok where material 

System must clearly track blind spots.

---

4. FanScore Stability

Check:

- distribution shape reasonable 

- no extreme clustering 

- no unexplained spikes 

- tier spread healthy 

- replay tests pass 

---

5. FitScore Behaviour

Validate:

- alignment signals behaving logically 

- no over-compression 

- no artificial inflation 

- budget gating working correctly 

---

6. Confidence Distribution Health

Healthy early profile typically shows:

- majority moderate confidence 

- growing high confidence 

- limited low-confidence concentration 

Warning signs:

- large low-confidence cluster 

- sport-specific blind spots 

- stale data accumulation 

---

7. Ingestion Reliability

Before scale, confirm:

- ingestion success rate stable 

- refresh cadence functioning 

- anomaly detection running 

- no systemic platform failures 

---

8. Explore Experience Sanity Check

Human review should confirm:

- rows feel credible 

- discovery feels balanced 

- no obvious distortions 

- no dominance by a few entities 

- mid-tier visibility present 

---

9. Trust Review

Final qualitative check:

Ask:

- Does this feel honest? 

- Does anything look over-precise? 

- Does Explore feel like a leaderboard? 

- Are we overstating coverage? 

If any answer is concerning → pause.

---

10. Go/No-Go Gate

SponsorAI should not scale publicly until:

- FanScore stable 

- FitScore explainable 

- confidence behaving 

- ingestion reliable 

- Explore credible 

Trust readiness outranks feature completeness.

---

End of Document
