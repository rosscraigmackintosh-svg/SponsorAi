## 1. SponsorAI — Explore Ranking Philosophy

Version: v1.0 

Last updated: 2026-02-23 

Authority: High (experience integrity)

---

1. Purpose

Defines how properties are surfaced and ordered in Explore while preserving SponsorAI’s intelligence-first stance.

Primary goal:

&gt; Enable discovery without creating winner/loser dynamics.

---

2. Core Principles

Explore must prioritise:

- legibility 

- diversity of discovery 

- signal clarity 

- deterministic behaviour 

- editorial restraint 

Explore must avoid:

- single ranked lists 

- “top performers” framing 

- optimisation theatre 

- excessive churn 

---

3. Row-Based Discovery Model

Explore uses **structured rows**, not a master ranking.

Canonical rows include:

- High Engagement Leaders 

- Fastest Growing 

- Emerging Opportunities 

- Established Premium Assets 

- Women’s Sport Spotlight 

- UK Market Focus 

- Sustainability &amp; Community Impact 

- Related to Your Watchlist 

Each row answers a different analytical question.

---

4. Ordering Within Rows

Within a row:

- ordering may use FanScore or row-specific logic 

- ordering must be deterministic 

- ordering must be explainable 

- ordering must avoid extreme volatility 

No row should imply:

&gt; “These are the best sponsorships.”

---

5. Property in Focus Rules

The hero surface must be:

- data-triggered 

- qualification-based 

- time-bounded 

- non-paid 

It must not feel editorial or promotional.

---

6. FitScore Interaction

FitScore may:

- rerank within filtered views 

- influence personalised discovery 

- support comparison workflows 

FitScore must not:

- override Explore structure 

- create global best-match ordering 

- collapse the three-signal model 

---

7. Freshness vs Stability Balance

Explore must balance:

- movement detection 

- ranking stability 

- user trust 

Guardrails:

- avoid excessive daily churn 

- suppress anomaly spikes 

- maintain recognisable ordering 

---

8. Transparency Expectations

Where helpful, the system may surface:

- qualification hints 

- row definitions 

- contextual notes 

But must avoid overwhelming the UI.

---

9. Future Guardrail

If Explore ever begins to feel like:

- a leaderboard 

- a betting table 

- an optimisation feed 

…it must be re-evaluated.

---

End of Document
