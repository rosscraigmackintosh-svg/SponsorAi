Section 10

Assistant Panel

Right-rail placement. Contained, calm, helpful, non-dominant.

AI Assistant
You
Why is the FitScore for Prema Racing higher than their FanScore?
Assistant
FanScore and FitScore measure different things. FanScore reflects audience attention across public channels. FitScore reflects how well the property's audience profile aligns with your brand criteria. A property can have a moderate FanScore but a strong FitScore if the audience is a close match, regardless of total scale.
