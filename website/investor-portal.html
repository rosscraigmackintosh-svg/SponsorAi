01

What SponsorAI Does

SponsorAI provides a structured view of the sponsorship landscape by analysing audience attention, engagement signals, and brand alignment across sports properties.

Instead of rankings or opinions, SponsorAI surfaces neutral signals that help organisations understand:

  • Where audience attention is growing
  • Which properties are building momentum
  • How different sponsorship opportunities compare
  • Where brand alignment may naturally exist

The platform helps decision-makers explore the sponsorship market with greater clarity and confidence.

02

What Makes It Different

SponsorAI is not a marketplace, an agency, or a brokerage. It is an intelligence platform designed to make the sponsorship market more transparent and easier to navigate.

—
No pay-to-rank
—
Neutral data signals
—
Structured market visibility
—
Decision support, not predictions

The goal is not to tell people what to sponsor. The goal is to make the landscape visible enough for better decisions to emerge.
